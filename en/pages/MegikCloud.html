Who is MegikPC?

MegikPC is a local company specialized in computer services, computer repairs, and sales of computer components. With extensive experience, we’ve become experts in providing reliable and secure IT solutions. We now also offer MegikCloud™, a high-quality and secure cloud service.

MegikCloud™ is a specialized cloud service offering security, privacy, and stability that large tech companies often overlook. With MegikCloud™, you have direct contact with us — the people taking care of your data. Our platform is tailored to the needs of local users, with a personalized approach and services.

MegikCloud™ uses Tailscale for secure access and data encryption, ensuring that only you can access your data. All your data remains protected, and only you have control over it. We cannot — and do not wish to — access your data, because your privacy matters.

  • Security: Uses Tailscale for encryption and data access available only to you.
  • Privacy: We do not access your data and never sell it.
  • Reliability: We use enterprise-grade Western Digital Gold drives for dependable data storage.
  • Personalized Service: Our team is always available to help you solve problems and provide support.

MegikCloud™ strictly follows all data privacy laws in Serbia. Your data is safe with us, and we will never access or sell your personal information. Our platform is designed so we physically cannot access your data — because protecting our clients' privacy is our priority.
